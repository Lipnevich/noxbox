Noxbox

Noxbox History

  • 2016 December

    The idea of convenient services

    We decided to use Google Cloud and to start working on mobile applications for Users and Performers

  • 2017 September

    Decision to use Bitcoin payments

    Seeing and understanding that the modern world is switching to crypto-currency payments, the most popular crypto currency in the world was chosen as the first payment system

  • 2018 March

    ICO

    We are here now

  • 2018 March - 2019 March
